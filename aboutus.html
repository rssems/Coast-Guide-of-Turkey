About Us

First of all, we would like to point out that the system you are in contains the editorial perspective, evaluation, and comments of our editors. The data presented is for information sharing purposes. Therefore, the System is not prepared for "Navigation" purposes.

The maps used on this site are derived from the products of the Navigation, Hydrography, and Oceanography Department (SHOD) with the permission of the SHOD Presidency (consisting of coastline, depth information, and cove/gulf/sea names). The SHOD Presidency is not responsible for other information added to the maps. These maps cannot be used for navigation purposes. For navigation maps, please contact the SHOD Presidency (www.shodb.gov.tr). Publishing rights belong to Coast Guide of Turkey, copyright belongs to the SHOD Presidency.
